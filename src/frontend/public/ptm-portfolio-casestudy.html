01 — Personas

Who Are We Designing For?

Two distinct user archetypes interact with the platform — each with very different needs, contexts, and emotional states.

👩‍🏫

Prof. Ananya Sharma

Design Faculty · 12 years experience · Age 38

40+Students/Sem
8Semesters
5Core Subjects

Goals

  • Present student data professionally
  • Save time during PTM sessions
  • Give parents full context on their child
  • Highlight both strengths and areas to improve

Pain Points

  • Manual marksheets are error-prone
  • Hard to include qualitative notes in print
  • No visual way to show skill balance
  • PTMs feel rushed and information-poor

Behaviours

  • Prepares data 2–3 days before PTM
  • Uses laptop or tablet during meetings
  • Prefers clean, structured data views

Tech Comfort

  • Moderate tech proficiency
  • Uses Google Sheets / Forms
  • Values simplicity over features
"I want parents to truly understand where their child stands — not just see a number."

Working Parents · Daughter in Sem 4 · Ages 42 & 40

1/yrPTM Attend
LowDesign Knowledge
HighAnxiety Level

Goals

  • Understand child's academic health
  • Know which subjects need attention
  • Feel confident and informed
  • Get a holistic picture, not just marks

Pain Points

  • Dense marksheets are hard to read
  • Only see numbers, not context
  • Short meeting time feels insufficient
  • No take-away material after the meeting

02 — Storyboard

The PTM Journey

Six scenes tracing the Professor's workflow — from preparation to the parent conversation.

SCENE 01 📅 PTM is Announced

Preparation Begins

Professor Ananya receives the PTM schedule. She has 3 days to compile marks, notes, and performance summaries for 40+ students across 5 subjects.

😟 Anxious
SCENE 02 🔐 Admin Login

Secure Data Entry

She logs into the PTM Architect admin panel using Internet Identity. The system recognises her as the registered Professor. She begins adding student records.

🎯 Focused
SCENE 03 📝 Data Input

Entering Marks & Notes

For each student, she fills marks per subject (out of 100), uploads their photo, writes detailed notes per subject, and adds the psychological context description.

🎯 Methodical
SCENE 04 🖥️ PTM Day — Presentation

The Meeting Begins

Parents sit across from Prof. Ananya. She pulls up the student's profile on her screen — the elegant cream and gold dashboard fills the display with the student's photo and summary.

💪 Confident
SCENE 05 📊 Analytics View

Visual Data Storytelling

She switches to the Analytics page. Animated radar and pie charts appear, showing skill balance and mark distribution. Parents immediately grasp areas of strength and weakness.

✨ Engaged
SCENE 06 🤝 Meeting Closes

Parents Leave Informed

Parents leave feeling heard and well-informed. Prof. Ananya returns to the admin panel to add the next student. The cycle continues — smoothly and efficiently.

🌟 Accomplished
